# Contract Summary — Galil Dairy Suppliers

**Contract ID:** STR-DAIRY-2025-014
**Supplier:** Galil Dairy Suppliers (Israel)
**Category:** Dairy — Grade A milk powder
**Effective Date:** 2025-09-01
**Term:** 24 months
**Renewal Date:** 2027-08-31 (auto-renews unless either party gives 90 days notice)

## Payment Terms
Net 45 from invoice date. 2% early payment discount if paid within 10 days.

## Volume Commitment
Minimum 18 tons/quarter, maximum 30 tons/quarter without prior written
agreement. Strauss has first-right-of-refusal on any volume above 30
tons/quarter before Galil offers it to other buyers.

## Pricing
Base price: ₪14.20/kg (approx. $3.85/kg at contract signing) for Grade A
milk powder, fixed for the contract term.

**Section 4.2 (Price Cap):** The base price shall not exceed ₪14.20/kg
during the 24-month contract term, except by mutual written amendment
signed by both parties. Market-driven price adjustments require minimum
30 days written notice and Strauss approval before taking effect.

## Penalty Clauses
- Late delivery beyond 5 business days: 1.5% of shipment value per day,
  capped at 15% of total shipment value.
- Quality failure (below Grade A spec on inspection): full replacement at
  supplier cost, no charge to Strauss.
