# Contract Summary — Golden Coast Cocoa Traders

**Contract ID:** STR-COCOA-2025-009
**Supplier:** Golden Coast Cocoa Traders (Ghana)
**Category:** Cocoa — Standard grade beans
**Effective Date:** 2025-03-01
**Term:** 18 months
**Renewal Date:** 2026-08-31

## Payment Terms
Net 45 from bill of lading date.

## Volume Commitment
Minimum 25 tons/quarter, maximum 45 tons/quarter. Volumes below minimum for two consecutive quarters trigger a contract review clause.

## Pricing
Base price: $2,780/ton at signing, subject to quarterly review against ICCO composite benchmark. Either party may request a price review with 15 days written notice; agreed adjustments require written confirmation from both sides before taking effect.

## Quality Acceptance Spec
Grade A cocoa beans defined as: moisture content <7.5%, defect rate <5% by weight. Supplier bears the pre-shipment inspection obligation using an SGS-accredited inspection body at origin port. Strauss conducts incoming quality inspection at destination; in the event of a discrepancy between origin and destination test results, an independent third-party test (agreed lab, split cost) is the binding resolution mechanism.

## Penalty Clauses
- Late delivery beyond 7 calendar days past agreed delivery window: 1.5% of shipment value per week delayed, capped at 12% of total shipment value.
- Short-shipment (delivered quantity below confirmed PO quantity): credit issued on next invoice for the shortfall at contract price.
- Quality failure at destination (confirmed by independent test): price reduction proportional to degree of non-conformance, or full replacement at supplier option.
