# Contract Summary — Ivoire Cacao Export

**Contract ID:** STR-COCOA-2025-002
**Supplier:** Ivoire Cacao Export (Côte d'Ivoire)
**Category:** Cocoa — Standard grade beans
**Effective Date:** 2025-06-01
**Term:** 12 months
**Renewal Date:** 2026-05-31

## Payment Terms
Net 30 from bill of lading date.

## Volume Commitment
Minimum 35 tons/quarter, maximum 50 tons/quarter. Volumes below minimum
for two consecutive quarters trigger a contract review clause.

## Pricing
Base price: $2,650/ton at signing, subject to quarterly review against
ICCO benchmark. Either party may request a price review with 15 days
notice; agreed adjustments require written confirmation from both sides.

## Penalty Clauses
- Delivery delay beyond agreed window: 1% of shipment value per week
  delayed, capped at 10%.
- Short-shipment (delivered quantity below confirmed PO quantity): credit
  issued on next invoice for the shortfall at contract price.
