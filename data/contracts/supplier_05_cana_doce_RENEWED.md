# Contract Summary — Cana Doce Açúcar (ACTIVE — renewed)

**Contract ID:** STR-SUGAR-2025-018
**Supplier:** Cana Doce Açúcar (Brazil)
**Category:** Sugar — refined white sugar (ICUMSA 45)
**Effective Date:** 2025-10-01
**Term:** 24 months
**Renewal Date:** 2027-09-30 (auto-renews for 12 months unless either party provides 90 days written notice of non-renewal)

## Payment Terms
Net 30 from bill of lading date.

## Volume Commitment
Minimum 55 tons/quarter, maximum 85 tons/quarter (increased from the prior contract).

## Pricing
$495/ton, fixed for the first 12 months of the contract term (through September 30, 2026). After 12 months, pricing subject to benchmark review against ICE No.11 futures with 30 days written notice required from either party for any proposed adjustment. Agreed adjustments require written confirmation from both parties before taking effect.

## Penalty Clauses
- Late delivery beyond agreed window: 1% of shipment value per week delayed, capped at 8%.
- Quality failure (ICUMSA reading exceeds 45 on independent test): full replacement shipment at supplier cost, no charge to Strauss.
